Seraphine International Hospital prides itself with ultra-modern facilities providing comprehensive medical care to everybody in need of health. It is manned by world class medical experts in several fields of medical practice. The hospital operates 24/7 with hygiene at a top class level.The hospital operates 24/7 with hygiene at a top class level. Seraphine operates a 24/7 pharmacy and ambulance services. Seraphine can boast of an ultra-modern laboratory and an Ultra-Sound and ECG unit. There is a canteen situated right on our premises.



Seraphine has three categories of wards for inpatients. They are: private wards, semi private wards, general wards. Each private ward has in it an air-conditioner, a fan, a table top fridge, a toilet and bathroom, etc and this ward is solely for an individual or a patient who wants to enjoy his or her privacy. There are two states-of- the- art theaters, an IVF unit, maternity or Labor Theater and ward, ICU, Consulting Rooms and many more. The hospital has dedicated resident doctors, in addition to a host of locums and other health specialists who come on scheduled / appointments times complimenting the dedicated and caring nurses.



The hospital has a car park for patients and visitors, subsequent to this; we have employed the services of security and traffic personnel to ensure the safety of vehicles.
